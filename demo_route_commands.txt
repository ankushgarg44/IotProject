Indoor demo route for Firebase realtime testing

This script is meant to verify the full app -> Firebase -> ESP32 path without going outside.

Sequence:
1. STRAIGHT after 1.5s
2. LEFT after 2.0s
3. STRAIGHT after 1.5s
4. RIGHT after 2.0s
5. STRAIGHT after 1.5s
6. UTURN after 2.5s

Firebase path used by the app:
bike_commands/direction

How to use:
- Open the app.
- Keep Wi-Fi and Firebase online.
- Press RUN DEMO ROUTE in Hardware Test Controls.
- Watch the Firebase Realtime Database value update step by step.
- Confirm the ESP32 reacts to each change.
